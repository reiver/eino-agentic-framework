Eino Agentic Framework

by Charles Iliya Krempeaux

Eino (pronounced similar to “I know”) is an open-source, production-grade agentic, LLM, and AI application framework.

You can more or less think of Eino as LangChain and LangGraph for the Go programming language (golang).

Eino was developed by ByteDance, and released under their CloudWeGo ecosystem.
